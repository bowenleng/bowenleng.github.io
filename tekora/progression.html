Progression & Gameplay in Tekora

Tekora is a Minecraft mod that introduces a tech tree in which systems are interconnected and progress is achieved through experimentation and partial exploration. The tech tree is divided into four main stages: the mech age, the steam age, the electric age, and the computer age. Each stage introduces new machinery and technologies that players can explore and utilize to advance their gameplay experience.

Gameplay

This is a basic gameplay guide for Tekora from the start to your first machine!

Before any machinery can be built, the first thing the player needs to do is to make a kiln furnace. To do so requires fire bricks which can be obtained by combining clay and bauxite, a new ore added by Tekora, to make fire clay before smelting it to make firebricks. Afterwards, the player has to craft some fire clay bricks to place in a 3x3 area and build upwards from there on out. The player can then place a kiln furnace controller on one side and some fire clay bricks around the pattern with a space in the middle. From that point onwards, the player would have a kiln furnace.

With the kiln furnace, the player has to combine 1 coal and 9 raw iron in the kiln furnace to make 9 steel ingots. With the steel ingots, the player can make a steel shaft which is the gateway to Tekora's mech-age progression.
